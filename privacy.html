MediCheck Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-10

MediCheck is an internal operational application used by authorized company personnel to complete equipment and vehicle inspection checklists.

Information We Collect

MediCheck may collect the following information solely for operational and documentation purposes:

  • User-provided inspection checklist responses
  • Photos captured within the app for inspection documentation
  • Basic identifiers such as unit number, inspection date, and inspector name or ID

MediCheck does not collect:

  • Personal consumer data
  • Location tracking data
  • Health or medical records
  • Data for advertising or marketing purposes

How Information Is Used

Collected information is used exclusively to document inspections, maintain internal compliance and audit records, and support operational review and reporting.

Information is not sold, shared for advertising, or used for tracking.

Data Storage and Security

Inspection data and photos are transmitted securely to company-managed infrastructure and stored in company-controlled systems. Access is restricted to authorized personnel.

Third-Party Services

MediCheck does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising networks, or data brokers.
